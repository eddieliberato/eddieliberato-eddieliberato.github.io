About Me

My name is Edi Liberato, aka jupt3r

I love to build stuff

In my professional career I have always been involved with prototyping. First making HVAC and Power steering system for a German auto parts manufacturer and later working with innovative special cables for a Swiss startup company.

I have a soft spot for computing, all sorts. I have a small collection of mechanical calculators and early programmable calculators. Recently I have been reading a lot about uncoventional computing and thermodynamic limits of computation.

My hobbies are woodworking and 3d printing. I believe craftsmanship, tools and machines are also forms of art and I like to take my time to admire that when I can.
